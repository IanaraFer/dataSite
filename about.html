Meet Ianara Fernandes

Founder & Data Scientist, Analytica Core AI

The Journey

From nursing to founder – Ianara's mission to make enterprise analytics accessible to every SME.

Ianara built Analytica Core AI out of necessity. As a full-time mom looking to re-enter the workforce after years in healthcare nursing, she discovered that her career couldn't accommodate the traditional 9-to-5. Instead of giving up, she learned data science—and struggled to find work without IT experience.

After countless rejections, she realized the real opportunity: why hire expensive data teams when a single platform could do everything in one place? She taught herself to build it. Today, she's back—and Analytica Core AI is the result of her determination to make enterprise analytics accessible to every business owner.

"My mission is to empower every SME with the data insights that used to be locked away by expensive consultants and large teams. Data is power—and it should be accessible to everyone."

Core Beliefs

Without data, you're guessing. With it, you're leading. Every business has data hiding inside it—it's just a matter of finding it.

Outcomes > Jargon

Anyone can make data complicated. True expertise is making it simple—and actionable. Your analysis should end with "what to do next", not "here's the pattern."

Trust Through Security

Your data is your competitive advantage. We treat it that way. GDPR compliant, encrypted, and never shared. Period.
